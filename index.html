    Our Story

    The idea behind PaperCoin was born several years ago after I saw a TV documentary about Plastiks.io. I was immediately fascinated by the concept of using blockchain to solve real-world waste problems.

    In 2022, I started working as a development engineer at a paper mill that processes 100% recycled paper. It didn’t take long for me to realize that high-quality wastepaper is becoming a rare commodity. The supply is shrinking, and demand keeps growing.

    In late 2024, I got back into the crypto world and came across educational content from Cryptoshäre. That’s when I started to truly understand how cryptocurrencies work – and how they can create real incentives.

    After several attempts on the Solana blockchain, I finally launched the PaperCoin project on the Base network in February 2025.

    PaperCoin is not just a token – it’s a mission. We want to reward recycling, support a circular economy, and make valuable raw materials more accessible through digital innovation.

    Roy Eggert

    Founder & CEO of PaperCoin

    Roadmap

    Our roadmap outlines a step-by-step expansion strategy through 2025–2027, including market growth, exchange listings, and pilot projects with paper factories and recycling companies.

    Phase 1: Value Growth & Market Capitalization (Q1 - Q3 2025)

    Goal: Increase the value of PaperCoin, build trust, and enhance liquidity.

    • Community Growth & Marketing
      • Intensive social media campaigns to raise awareness.
      • Partnerships with crypto influencers and environmental activists.
      • Educational initiatives on the economic and ecological benefits of PaperCoin.
    • Partnerships with Crypto Projects & DeFi Platforms
      • Listing on well-known DEXs (Decentralized Exchanges) to increase liquidity.
      • Introduction of staking options for passive income opportunities.
      • Collaborations with sustainable blockchain projects.
    • Expansion to Larger Exchanges & Tier-2 Listings
      • Listing PaperCoin on centralized exchanges to attract more investors.
      • Collaboration with market makers to stabilize pricing.

    Phase 2: Tier-1 Exchange Listing & Initial Corporate Partnerships (Q4 2025)

    Goal: Secure a listing on a leading crypto exchange and establish the first industry partnerships.

    • Tier-1 Exchange Listing
      • Application for a listing on Binance, Coinbase, or Kraken.
      • Increasing market capitalization through strategic investor engagement.
      • Positioning PaperCoin as a sustainable ESG investment.
    • Introduction to the Paper Industry
      • Presenting PaperCoin as a pilot project in your paper factory.
      • Engaging with other paper factories for potential integration.
      • Initial testing of PaperCoin as a payment method for waste paper suppliers.

    Phase 3: Pilot Projects & Initial Real-World Applications (Q1 - Q3 2026)

    Goal: Establish PaperCoin in the industry and launch real-world use cases.

    • Pilot Projects with Recycling Companies & Waste Paper Traders
      • Testing PaperCoin as a rewards system for waste paper deliveries.
      • Developing a payment system for waste paper transactions.
      • Collaborating with packaging and printing companies for integration into sustainable supply chains.
    • Launch of a Digital Marketplace for Waste Paper
      • A platform where traders and citizens can use PaperCoin for waste paper transactions.
      • Real-time pricing based on global market data.
      • Introduction of an automated smart contract system for secure transactions.

    Phase 4: Mass Adoption & Global Expansion (Q4 2026 - 2027)

    Goal: Establish PaperCoin as the standard for the waste paper trade and sustainable economic cycles.

    • International Expansion
    • PaperCoin is accepted in multiple countries as a payment method for recycling.
    • Partnerships with environmental organizations and governments.
    • Further development as a sustainable financial instrument.
    • Implementation of a DAO System (Decentralized Autonomous Organization)
    • Token holders can vote on key decisions.
    • Sustainability Fund: A portion of transaction fees is allocated to environmental projects.
