Vision

The sustainable use of waste paper is a key component of a resource-efficient circular economy. Our goal is to establish an innovative token-based reward system that ensures waste paper is efficiently and economically made available to paper mills worldwide.

The price of waste paper fluctuates significantly, which directly affects the production costs and economic viability of the paper industry. To address these challenges, we continuously analyze price developments and ensure that our token serves as a stabilizing element in the value chain.
